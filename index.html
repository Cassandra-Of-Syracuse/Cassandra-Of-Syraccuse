Onion dip and chips

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: for 5 servings
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 400˚F (200˚C).

Step 2: Spread the onions on a baking sheet and drizzle with the olive oil. Roast for 15-20 minutes, until the onion is slightly charred.

Step 3: Let cool to room temperature, then roughly chop and transfer to a medium bowl.

Step 4: To the bowl, add the mayonnaise, sour cream, spice blend, salt, and lemon juice and whisk to combine.

Step 5: Serve the onion dip with chips and enjoy!

Classic party guacamole

  • Time: 5-10 minutes
  • Serves: for 8 servings
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: In a medium bowl, combine the avocado, tomato, red onion, jalapeño, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix together until fully incorporated. Top with cilantro, if using.

Step 2: Serve with tortilla chips.

Step 3: Enjoy!
